Hobbies and Interests!

I'm glad you're curious about my hobbies and interests!

I hope you'll find me fascinating!




Speedcubing

Ever since the winter of 2018, I have been fascinated by the world of competitive Rubik's Cube solving.
As of June 2024, I have attended 16 competitions, completing 500+ solves in 11 different events.
I specialize in the traditional 3x3x3 (PR: 6.72 seconds) and the Pyraminx event (PR: 2.12 seconds).

In January 2023, my average of 5 solves in the Pyraminx event ranked me #73 in the United States and #9 in California.
Check out my cubing records above!

Music

In my free time, I love playing the music I love, such as Billy Joel's Piano Man or Elton John's Rocket Man.

Baseball

I can never pass up a chance to root for the Dodgers!
Ever since I was introduced to Baseball when I was 9, I have been an avid fan of the sport ever since!

Go Blue!

Track & Field

I have been a hurdler on my school's Varsity Track & Field team for 3 years now!
I specialize in the 110m (PR: 17.75) and 300m hurdles (PR: 46.36).
I am most proud of being a member of the 4x110m Shuttle Hurdle Relay team, where we have held the school record 3 times!

When I have a few minutes to spare, I love sending a game or two to my friends to play via GamePigeon! I can never pass up a game of Word Hunt, Mancala, or Gomoku.
